About Me
I am a web developer/designer with 6 years of industry experience, I worked in London for 2 years and Australia for 4 years, Working on a wide range of clients.

I pride myself on honesty and providing my clients with the best possible customer care and advice. If you are after an affordable website that is designed specifically for your needs, then I am the guy for the job.
During my time I have developed high end e-commerce solutions (shopping cart sites) to interactive websites that bring brand awareness. I am based in Canada, Vancouver but have clients and contacts world wide.

The internet is loaded with traffic trying to find a product or service and it is a great source for potential customers to find local businesses.
Having a website is one of the cheapest methods of advertising and can reach people world wide. In europe alone there are over 425,773,571 people that use the internet and in Oceania / Australia there is 21,110,490 use the internet thats 60.8 % of the population.

Imagine opening the door to the world and therefore increasing clientele.
